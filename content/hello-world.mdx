## Entering the bloggin world

I've been thinking about starting a blog for a while now, but I never had the courage to do it. For me, every bit of
knowledge, especially in the tech world, should be shared. <br />

I've been doing this for a while with my colleagues, but I think it's time to expand a little bit more and share here
as well! <br />

I'm not sure if the approach of creating a personal blog, instead of using a platform like Medium, is the best one,
so I'm doing both! I highly encourage you to check my profile over [Medium](https://gabrielcampospc.medium.com/) as well!
